PAYMENT OPTIONS

Financial

Incentive program for all clients accruing weekly and awarded after 6 months in good standing with MRTR.

Communication

Clients and individual house managers are in direct contact with treatment centers, Certified Peer Support and Lead House Manager.

Service Work

MRTR is provided with many ways to involve clients for service projects within the community.

Schedule

Weekly house meetings and recovery meeting cards. Teaching clients healthy communication and commitment to their recovery.

Health

MRTR has information relating to health care services needed for treatment, IOP, mental health and TAM.

Anger Management

Two Certified Peer Support Specialist accessible to all clients to resolve conflict. MRTR can also refer you to a therapist for consultation for any mental health needs.
